# Stele

A small, opinionated operations system built on event sourcing and bi-temporal data.
The thesis: the business is its history of events; current state is just a projection.

Phase 1 vertical: warranty management for a single small business. Greenfield, MIT licensed.

- [VISION.md](./VISION.md) — what and why

- [docs/adr/](./docs/adr/) — architectural decision records

## Status

Inception. Not deployable yet. See ROADMAP M0.

## Why "Stele"?

A stele is an upright stone slab bearing an inscription. The Rosetta Stone is a stele.
What is written on it cannot be unwritten, only added to. The metaphor is the foundation.
